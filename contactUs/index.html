About US

The Sparks Luminaires has been founded by Mr. Sanjay Sharma 2012 with the aim to provide immense customer satisfaction by providing them exclusive product quality and after sales service.

We are a professional lighting company whose differentiation lies in the quality of its Our Our Products and customer contentment. The company is involved in manufacturing, all kinds of decorative and ornamental, lighting poles, facade, lighting, and all kinds of landscape lighting.
